Lightning Fast Performance

Experience fast message delivery without excessive delays. Specifically designed for large-scale blasting needs with a stable and efficient delivery optimization system. The application runs directly on your computer, ensuring more responsive performance and no reliance on external servers.

  • Fast & controlled delivery process
  • Stable system for large volume shipments
  • Automatic delay & batch settings
  • Real-time campaign data synchronization

Professional Level Security System

Your contact and campaign data are valuable assets. Therefore, this app is designed with a multi-layered security system to maintain privacy and stability. All data is stored on your device, giving you complete control without relying on third-party servers.

  • Secure local data storage
  • Access protection with a licensing system
  • Do not share or store databases on external servers
  • Regular security system updates
  • Delivery validation to minimize the risk of errors & spam

Flexible & Easy to Integrate

Designed to be compatible with a wide range of modern business needs, this application runs stably in a variety of environments and easily integrates with your existing work systems. The architectural structure is made adaptive, so it is ready to follow the development of your business needs in the future.

  • Compatible with various versions of Windows
  • Import data from Excel & CSV easily
  • Supports various SMTP services
  • Integrated with WhatsApp system for mass sending
  • Modular architecture ready to be developed

Informative Campaign Monitoring & Reporting

Monitor the performance of each shipment with clear, easy-to-understand data. The app provides reports that help you evaluate campaign effectiveness and make more informed decisions. All statistics can be accessed directly from the dashboard in real-time.

  • Delivery statistics (successful, failed, pending)
  • Email tracking (open & click, if using active tracking)
  • WhatsApp delivery report
  • Campaign history is saved automatically
  • Compact & easy to read dashboard display

Easy Integration with Your Business Workflow

Designed to work with your existing systems and work processes. Import data, set up campaigns, and manage deliveries without changing your existing business flow. Flexible for both individual and team needs.

  • Import data from Excel & CSV
  • Supports various SMTP services

  • Modular system structure & easy to expand
  • Support for regular feature updates
